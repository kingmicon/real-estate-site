Frequently Ask Question

This are the most frequently ask Question

What methods of payment are supported?

▼

We accept various payment methods, including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and online payment platforms. For larger transactions, we also support escrow services to ensure secure and transparent processing. Please contact our sales team for more information on your preferred payment method.

Can I cancel at any time?

▼

Cancellation policies depend on the specific terms of your purchase agreement. Generally, cancellations are allowed within a specified period after the contract is signed, but there may be associated fees. We recommend reviewing the contract carefully or contacting our customer service team to understand your options.

How do I get a receipt for my purchase?

▼

Once your payment is processed, a receipt will be automatically emailed to you. If you require a physical copy or if you need to access your receipt at a later date, you can also download it from your account on our website or request it through our customer support team.

Which licenses do I need?

▼

The licenses required depend on the type of property and its intended use. For residential properties, typically no special licenses are needed beyond the standard purchase agreement. However, for commercial properties or specific types of development, you may need additional permits or licenses. Our team can guide you through the process and help you obtain any necessary documentation.

How do I get access to a property I purchased?

▼

After the final payment and completion of all required paperwork, you will receive the keys to the property, along with any necessary access codes or instructions. Our team will schedule a convenient time for the handover and can assist you with any questions or concerns during the process.
